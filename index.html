Frequently Asked Questions

What is your Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)?

+

Our MOQ is flexible, starting from 5 cubic meters (cbm) for Less than Container Load (LCL) shipments. This allows you to start with smaller quantities and scale up to Full Container Loads (FCL) as your business grows.

Do you offer private labeling and custom packaging?

+

Absolutely. We have extensive experience with over 139 white labelling projects. We can work with you to create custom branding, logos, and market-ready packaging that reflects your brand's identity.

How do you ensure product quality?

+

We have a rigorous quality control process. This includes an early-stage inspection during manufacturing and a final, comprehensive pre-shipment check to ensure every product meets the approved specifications before it leaves India.

Can you consolidate products from multiple suppliers in India?

+

Yes, this is one of our core services. We offer both supplier and freight consolidation, allowing you to source from multiple factories and ship everything in a single, optimized container. This simplifies logistics and reduces costs.
